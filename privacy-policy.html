Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 05, 2026

1. Introduction

At Krushi Kalp, we are committed to providing a reliable mock test platform for government exam preparation. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and use your data to ensure service integrity and user support.

2. Google Authentication & Data Integrity

We utilize Google Sign-In to ensure that every user on our platform is authentic. By using Google Authentication, we prevent the creation of accounts with "fake data" and maintain a high standard of data integrity across our testing platform. We access your:

  • Email Address: Primarily used for account verification, login, and secure identification.
  • Full Name: To personalize your experience and certificates.
  • Profile Picture: For account identification within the app interface.

We do not access your contacts, files, or any other private data from your Google account. This information is stored securely in our database managed via Supabase.

3. Data Usage for Support & Payments

  • Verification: Your email is used to verify your identity during login.
  • Payment Troubleshooting: We collect your mobile number and email to assist you in case of payment crashes or failures. This allows you to easily share transaction screenshots or documentation via our support channels for resolution.
  • Progress Tracking: Storing your scores and performance metrics.

4. Data Residency & Security

All user data is stored on secure cloud servers managed by Supabase. We implement Industry-standard encryption and Row-Level Security (RLS) policies to ensure that only you can access your personal exam data.
