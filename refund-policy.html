Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: February 05, 2026

1. Cancellation Policy

Since Krushi Kalp provides digital goods (Mock Tests), once a purchase is made and the content is accessed or the test is started, the order cannot be cancelled.

2. Refund Policy

We offer a 7-day refund policy for purchases made through our platform under the following conditions:

  • The content was not accessible due to technical issues from our end.
  • The purchase was made erroneously and the content has NOT been accessed or viewed.

To request a refund, please contact us at our support email with your transaction ID and details of the issue. Refunds will be processed within 5-7 working days to the original payment method.

3. Exceptional Cases

Refunds may be granted at the sole discretion of the management for exceptional cases. Decisions made by the Krushi Kalp management will be final.
